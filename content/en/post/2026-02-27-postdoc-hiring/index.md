### 招聘启事：上海交通大学矩阵光学实验室博士后

上海交通大学**矩阵光学实验室（Matrix Optics Laboratory）**现诚聘 **2名** 博士后，诚邀海内外优秀青年学者加盟。

* **研究方向**：光学成像、量子光学、深度学习、光计算等相关领域。
* **薪酬待遇**：年薪 **34万人民币**。实验室将提供一流的科研平台、充足的经费支持及广阔的职业发展空间。
* **应聘方式**：有意向者请将个人简历发送至实验室负责人 **苗鹏 副教授** 邮箱：[pengmiao@sjtu.edu.cn](mailto:pengmiao@sjtu.edu.cn)。

### Recruitment: Postdoctoral Fellow Positions at Matrix Optics Laboratory

The **Matrix Optics Laboratory** at Shanghai Jiao Tong University is seeking **2 motivated Postdoctoral Fellows** to join our team.

* **Research Areas**: Optical Imaging, Quantum Optics, Deep Learning, Optical Computing, and related fields.
* **Compensation**: A competitive salary of **340,000 RMB per year** will be provided. We offer a vibrant research environment with cutting-edge facilities and ample funding support.
* **How to Apply**: Interested candidates should send their CV and a brief statement of research interests to the Principal Investigator, **Associate Professor Peng Miao**, at [pengmiao@sjtu.edu.cn](mailto:pengmiao@sjtu.edu.cn).
